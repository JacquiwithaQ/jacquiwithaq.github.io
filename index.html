I'm Jacqui Fashimpaur,
software developer and computational artist.

I'm a senior at Carnegie Mellon University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a minor in Film and Media Studies.

I'm passionate about understanding and improving the ways people interact with technology, both for utility and entertainment. I make art projects exploring the places where the physical and digital worlds meet.

I serve as co-president of Puzzlehunt CMU and presdient of CMU's Film Club. I also am a head teaching assistant for the course Great Ideas in Theoretical Computer Science. In my free time, I like to learn about a variety of subjects, including mathematics, film theory, and psychology. I also enjoy playing board games, painting with watercolors, watching movies, and making digital games and art projects.
