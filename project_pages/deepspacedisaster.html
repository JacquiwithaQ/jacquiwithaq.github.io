Matt & Emma's Deep-Space Disaster
2025 · Figma, Adobe Suite
An online puzzlehunt integrated with an interactive sci-fi comic.
Matt and Emma's Deep-Space Disaster is the third Teammate Hunt, an online puzzlehunt organized by the group teammate. It contains around fifty puzzles, including unusual crosswords, logic puzzles, and interactive games, and it was designed for teams of four to eight people. In the week it was active, 147 teams reached an ending and 603 teams solved at least one puzzle. The hunt's story features our recurring characters, twins Matt and Emma, this time on board a malfunctioning spaceship. The puzzles are embedded in a comic that expands to tell the twins' story depending on the solvers' choices. Mouse over to reveal spoilers: the twins will always die on the first attempt, but they are revealed to have time travel capabilities. Teams must reset to the beginning of the story several times and explore possible decisions in order to gather the info necessary to complete all the puzzles. For now, puzzles in Deep-Space Disaster are only accessible to logged-in teams who have unlocked them. I encourage you to register a team if you're interested in playing through the hunt.

The hunt's promotional "cover page."

This puzzlehunt was created over one year by about thirty-five members of teammate. I pitched the idea and served on the hunt's executive board as Creative Lead. In this role I managed the hunt's art and story, including integrating the puzzle structure with the plot, designing many parts of the website and comic, and organizing work for the art team (about six people). I also co-wrote five puzzles in the hunt — Stock→Pile, Pangrams, Paingram, Built to Spec, and Plot a Course — and edited many more. Lots of behind-the-scenes details can be found in the hunt's wrap-up post. See my Puzzle CV for more puzzles I've written.
