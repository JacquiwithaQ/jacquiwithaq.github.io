GAN-generated stock photos of salads.
DeepStock is a series of generative stock photos of salads, made to explore issues of originality and ownership in online content creation. To generate them, I trained an existing Pytorch representation of DCGAN on a dataset of Shutterstock photos labeled "salad." A timelapse of the images it generated while training is below. To improve the resolution of the resulting images, I created a Pix2Pix model specifically to sharpen and texturize salad photos. The final photos had clearly defined bowls, leaves, and watermarks.


DeepStock was my part of a group project called "Deep Pix2Stock Salad," in which Angela Liang, Anirudh Mani, and I explored multiple methods of generating salad images. The project was made for the course Art and Machine Learning, taught by Professors Eunsu Kang and Barnabas Poczos. Our full documentation is available here.
